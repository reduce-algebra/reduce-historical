AIRY_AI _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ operator

The Airy_Ai operator returns the Airy Ai function for a given argument.

syntax:

Airy_Ai(<argument>)

examples: 


on complex;
on rounded;
Airy_Ai(0); 


  0.355028053888          


Airy_Ai(3.45 + 17.97i); 

  - 5.5561528511e+9 - 8.80397899932e+9*i
