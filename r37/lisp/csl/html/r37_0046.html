FIRST _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ operator

The first operator returns the first element of a list.

syntax:

first(<list>) or first <list>

<list> must be a non-empty list to avoid an error message.

examples:


alist := {a,b,c,d};

  ALIST := {A,B,C,D}


first alist;

  A


blist := {x,y,{ww,aa,qq},z};

  BLIST := {X,Y,{WW,AA,QQ},Z}


first third blist;

  WW
