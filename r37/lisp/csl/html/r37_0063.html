SECOND _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ operator

The second operator returns the second element of a list.

syntax:

second(<list>) or second <list>

<list> must be a list with at least two elements, to avoid an error message.

examples:


alist := {a,b,c,d};

  ALIST := {A,B,C,D}


second alist;

  B


blist := {x,{aa,bb,cc},z};

  BLIST := {X,{AA,BB,CC},Z}


second second blist;

  BB
