COLUMN_DIM _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ operator

Column dimension, row dimension:

syntax:

column_dim(<matrix>)

<matrix> :- a matrix.

column_dimfinds the column dimension of <matrix>.

row_dimfinds the row dimension of <matrix>.

examples: 



column_dim(A); 

  3 


row_dim(A); 

  3
