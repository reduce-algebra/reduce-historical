JACOBIP _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ operator

The JacobiP operator computes the nth Jacobi Polynomial.

syntax:

JacobiP(<integer>,<expression>,<expression>, <expression>)

examples:


JacobiP(3,4,5,xx); 

          3         2
  7*(65*xx   - 13*xx   - 13*xx + 1)
  ----------------------------------
                  8



JacobiP(3,4,5,6); 

  94465/8
