RATPRI INDEX

RATPRI _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ switch

When the ratpri switch is on, rational expressions and fractions are printed as two lines separated by a fraction bar, rather than in a linear style. Default is on.

examples: 


3/17; 

  3
  -- 
  17


2/b + 3/y; 

  3*B + 2*Y
  --------- 
     B*Y


off ratpri; 

3/17; 

  3/17 


2/b + 3/y; 

  (3*B + 2*Y)/(B*Y)
