ROUND INDEX

ROUND _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ operator

syntax:

round(<expression>)

If its argument has a numerical value, round rounds it to the nearest integer. For non-numeric arguments, the value is an expression in the original operator.

examples: 


round 3.4; 

  3 


round 3.5; 

  4 


round a; 

  ROUND(A)
