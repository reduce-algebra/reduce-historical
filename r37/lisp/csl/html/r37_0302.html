LIST _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ switch

The list switch causes REDUCE to print each term in any sum on separate lines.

examples: 


x**2*(y**2 + 2*y) + x*(y**2 + z)/(2*a);
			 


            2              2
  X*(2*A*X*Y  + 4*A*X*Y + Y  +Z)
  ------------------------------ 
               2*A


on list; 

ws;

  (X*(2*A*X*Y
    + 4*A*X*Y
       2
    + Y
    + Z))/(2*A)
