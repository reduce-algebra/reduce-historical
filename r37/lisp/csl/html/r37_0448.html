EULERP INDEX

EULERP _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ operator

The EulerP operator returns the nth Euler Polynomial.

syntax:

EulerP(<integer>,<expression>)

examples:


EulerP(2,xx);

  xx*(xx - 1)


EulerP(10,3);

  2046

The Euler numbers are the values of the Euler Polynomials at 1/2 multiplied by 2**n.
